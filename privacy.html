Privacy Policy

This app accesses only the account holder's own TikTok account data (video metadata, view counts, like counts, share counts) for the purpose of monitoring channel performance.

What we collect

  • Video metadata (titles, durations, IDs) for videos posted by the authenticated account
  • Engagement counts (views, likes, comments, shares) for those videos

What we do not collect

  • Personal data from any other TikTok user
  • Data from accounts the authenticated user follows or interacts with
  • Any data not directly attributable to the authenticated account's own content

How data is stored

Collected data is stored locally on the account holder's machine. It is not transmitted to any external service except TikTok's own API. No third-party analytics or tracking is used.

Sharing

No data is shared with any third party.
