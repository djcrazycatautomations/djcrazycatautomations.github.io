DJCC Analytics

A single-account internal tool used by the DJCC channel owner to read performance metrics from their own TikTok account via TikTok's Display API.

What it does

  • Reads metrics (views, likes, comments, shares) for the authenticated account's own videos
  • Stores results locally to inform future content decisions
  • Does not collect, store, or transmit data from any other user

What it is not

  • Not a public service
  • Not a SaaS product
  • Not shared with third parties
